Making a Contribution

Please complete the details on this form. If you like, you can choose to have your name displayed and a short message shown here. Your email will be kept private.

Once submitted, you will then be redirected to the Australian Communities Foundation website to complete the payment in their system.

Allocation Instructions

By default, your gift goes to the whole fund and will be allocated across all the causes through matching.

Alternatively, you may elect to nominate specific causes you would like your gift to go to. The fund will match dollar-for-dollar, while there is money remaining in the matching pool.

How to select specific causes

Please use the drop down menu to select a cause. Then enter the amount you would like to give to this cause. Press the + button to add.

You can repeat this process as often as you like. If you make a mistake, click on the rubbish bin icon to delete that row.

This is where the submit status goes Please continue to the Australian Communities Foundation website to complete the donation. Remember to provide the same email address there to ensure your instructions are followed.
